🧐 The Curiosity Game

👥 Two people: A (Questioner) & B (Receiver)

Who starts? → The person with longer hair

📜 Basic Structure

1️⃣ A asks B (2 min)

  • ✅ Ask questions you are genuinely curious about.
  • ❌ Don't ask what you think you're "supposed" to ask.
  • ❌ Avoid pre-planned or societal questions.

💡 Tips for B (Receiver):

  • Try to feel the other person’s curiosity.
  • You may answer or not answer – either is okay!

2️⃣ B gives feedback to A (1 min)

  • 🔹 What feedback do you have on A’s questions?
  • 🔹 What do you wish A had asked?

3️⃣ A continues, incorporating feedback (1 min)

  • ✅ 1 more question → *Using what you learned from feedback*
  • ✅ 1 reflection sentence stem

💬 Reflection sentence stems (A → B):

  • "My first impression of you was..."
  • "The moment I felt you the most was..."
  • "What I really get about you is..."

🔄 Swap roles & repeat!

Make curiosity a practice. 🎭
